  - type: table

      - file.name

      - author

      - property: file.mtime

        - status == "todo"

      - file.name

## Logs

```dataview
TASK
WHERE (icontains(text, "#log/read")) AND 
		!(icontains(text, "#task") OR icontains(text, "#question"))
	AND !completed
GROUP BY file.name as Filename
SORT fows.file.ctime ASC
LIMIT 20
```
